What this means

As a ‘Care experienced person’, you do not qualify for a care leaver status based on the time you spent in care.

Depending on your local council, you may still get some support.

Support you could be entitled to

It’s up to your local council what support they provide to you.

If you came to the UK to seek asylum, you could also be entitled to additional support as an asylum-seeking person.
