Who this service is for

You may have the right to support if you are between 15 and 25 and:

  • have been in care (for example, in foster care, residential care or supported lodgings)
  • have been in custody (for example, in a secure children's home, secure training centre or young offender institution)
  • came the UK on your own (to seek asylum).
