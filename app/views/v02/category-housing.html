Support for leaving care

As someone leaving care, you may have legal rights to extra support.

What you get depends on the time you spent in care and who your local council is.

Local support

Extra help your local council may offer you through their ‘Local Offer’

Advanced claims for Universal Credit

You might be able to apply for Universal Credit early to get in time for leaving care at 18
