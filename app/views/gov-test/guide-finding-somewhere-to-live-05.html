Help with housing costs

If you’re 16 or 17

If you’re 16 or 17, your council must pay for your housing costs, like rent, bills and other housing expenses.

If you’re 18 or over

If you’re 18 or over, you’ll have to pay your own housing costs, but you may be able to get housing support.
