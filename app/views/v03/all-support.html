What do you need help with?

Money and benefits

Help with money when you leave care and benefits you can apply for

Housing and accommodation

Help finding somewhere to live and other support with housing costs

Work and employment

Support and services to help with finding and staying in work
 

Education and training

Support and services to help you start or continue education and training

Health and wellbeing

Help with your physical and mental health, including disability support

People who support you

People that help you prepare for leaving care and support you along the way

Caring for others

Help with childcare or support if you are someone’s carer
 

Coming to the UK

Help if you came to the UK to seek asylum (to be safe)
