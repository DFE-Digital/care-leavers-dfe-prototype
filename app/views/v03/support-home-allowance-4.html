How and when you'll get it

Ask your personal advisor or someone from your local council to apply for a Care Leaver Grant when you turn 18.

Once the local council agrees on the amount, they'll pay it into your bank account.

You may get the full amount in one go or over a few months. This depends on your personal advisor and local council.

If you're in a 'stay put' arrangement, you can apply at 18. But you may decide to receive the payment when you move out or turn 21.
