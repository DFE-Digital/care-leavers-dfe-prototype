What this means

As a "Qualifying child", you’re entitled to support because you:

  • are still in care
  • are between 16 and 17
  • spent more than 13 weeks in care since the age of 14 — this includes time after you turn 16.

Support you’re entitled to

Most of the support you’ll be entitled to when you turn 18 is provided by your local council.

If you came to the UK to seek asylum, you could also be entitled to additional support as an asylum-seeking person.
