Check what support you're entitled to

What support you’re entitled to depends on your care leaver status.

This is based on how long you were in care and when, as well as your age.

Who this service is for

You can use this service if you’re 15 to 25 and have been:

  • in foster care
  • in residential care
  • under special guardianship
  • in custody (for example, in a secure children's home, secure training centre or young offender institution)
  • an unaccompanied asylum-seeking child or young person (UASC).
