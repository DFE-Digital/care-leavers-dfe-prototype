Who is entitled to it

Whether you're entitled to this depends care leaver status (this is based on time you have spent in care).

A former relevant child is entitled to this.

If you are a relevant child or eligible child, you will be eligible for this when you turn 18.

What care leavers allowance is

Care leavers allowance is a payment you get when you leave care.

It's sometimes called 'setting up home allowance'.

It helps you buy stuff for your home.

How much you'll get

Minimum of 3K

How much you get depends on your local council and level of need

How and when you'll get it

Your PA should set this up for you

You get it when you turn 18

You get it over 2 payments annually, as equal in amount as possible

If you're receiving other support

It is your legal right to receive this support

You should get this support even if

  • you are already receiving benefits or other financial support
  • you are in a 'stay put' arrangement

Issues with this support

If you never recieved this support, speak to your personal advisor or someone from your local council.

It is your legal right to get this support if you're entitled to it.
