Needs assessment

You have the right to (you’re entitled to) a ‘needs assessment’.

This is where your local council talks with you to find out and agree what support and advice you need.

Education and training support

If you’re at college or university full time, your council might give you support like:

  • accommodation during term holidays
  • help with costs like course fees or equipment

Talk to someone from your council to find out more.

More support

There is more help you could get that:

  • is for anyone with care experience
  • you can apply for, like benefits or student finance

Help with nationality
We need to know your nationality so we can work out which elections you’re entitled to vote in. If you cannot provide your nationality, you’ll have to send copies of identity documents through the post.
