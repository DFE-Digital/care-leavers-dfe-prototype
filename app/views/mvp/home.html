Who is this support for?

You might have the right to leaving care support if you:

  • are aged 16 to 24
  • were looked after or have spent time in care in England

This includes:

  • foster care
  • residential care (like a children’s home)
  • time in custody (like a young offender institution or secure children’s home)
  • if you came to seek asylum in the UK alone before 18
